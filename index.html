If You Build it...

It increases your metabolic rate, which causes your body to burn more fat.

No matter how young or old you are you will have more energy if you build more muscle. After shedding excess fat there will be a lot less of you to carry around.

Furthermore body building is absolutely crucial to an effective weight loss program. This is because when you lose weight through dieting it is possible to lose muscle. To keep the weight off and also to keep from wasting away you also need to be on a complimentary program of muscle building along with your aerobic program of exercise which may include such activities as swimming, jogging or spinning on a stationary bicycle.
